Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical object to harbor life. It is the fifth largest planet in our solar system and the largest of the four rocky, terrestrial planets.

Key characteristics of Earth include:
*   **Composition and Surface:** Earth is a rocky planet with a solid and dynamic surface featuring mountains, canyons, plains, and valleys. Approximately 71% of its surface is covered by water, making it an "ocean world".
*   **Atmosphere:** Its atmosphere is primarily composed of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%), which is a perfect balance for supporting life.
*   **Orbit and Rotation:** Earth orbits the Sun in approximately 365.25 days, and it rotates on its axis in about 23 hours and 56 minutes. This rotation causes day and night, while the tilt of its axis creates seasons.
*   **Moon:** Earth has one natural satellite, the Moon, which orbits at an average distance of about 384,400 km (238,900 mi).
*   **Age:** Earth is believed to have formed around 4.5 billion years ago.
*   **Habitability:** Earth's unique combination of accessible liquid water, an oxygen-rich atmosphere, and stable temperatures makes it the only known planet to support life.
